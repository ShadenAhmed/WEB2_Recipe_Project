Multi-Grain Power Loaf
  • 3 cups whole wheat flour
  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • 1 tbsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ cup mixed seeds (pumpkin, sunflower, flax)
  • ¼ cup dried cranberries
  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  1. Mix: Combine dry ingredients and seeds. Gradually add water, honey, and oil.
  2. Knead: Knead for 5-8 minutes until smooth. Cover and let rise in a warm place for 1 hour.
  3. Shape: Form into an oval loaf. Brush the top with water and press extra seeds/oats onto the surface.
  4. Second Rise: Let it rest for 20 minutes. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  5. Bake: Score the top with a knife. Bake for 30-40 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Cool: Allow to cool completely before slicing.

Flaxseed & Quinoa Bread
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • ½ cup ground flaxseeds
  • 1 cup chickpea flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • water (adjust as needed)
  1. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, mix the chickpea flour, ground flaxseeds, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Add Grains: Fold in the cooked quinoa until evenly distributed.
  3. Mix Liquids: Add the apple cider vinegar, then gradually pour in water while stirring until you reach a thick, scoopable batter consistency.
  4. Shape: Place the dough into a greased loaf pan or shape it into rustic rolls (like in the image).
  5. Bake: Preheat your oven to 180°C and bake for about 45 minutes (or until the crust is firm and sounds hollow when tapped).
  6. Cool: Let it rest on a wooden surface until completely cool before slicing.

Oat & Banana Muffins
  • 2 ripe bananas (mashed)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp honey (or maple syrup)
  • 1.5 cups oat flour (or quick oats blended into flour)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  1. Prep Wet: In a bowl, mash bananas. Whisk in eggs and honey.
  2. Prep Dry: In a separate bowl, combine oat flour, baking powder, and cinnamon.
  3. Combine: Add dry ingredients to wet, mix until just combined (do not overmix).
  4. Bake: Pour batter into greased muffin tins. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
